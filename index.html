About NO SHOW


Established in May 2016, NO SHOW Comedy, LLC is a live arts and entertainment business presenting a variety of comedic talent.
Based out of Baton Rouge, Louisiana, NO SHOW proudly celebrates its first anniversary! The show began as a monthly booked feature series showcasing both local and national stand up performances. In the past year, NO SHOW expanded its flagship feature event to offer two charity shows, a holiday comedy roast, and aNOther SHOW-- a separate, weekly open mic with select branded artwork.
